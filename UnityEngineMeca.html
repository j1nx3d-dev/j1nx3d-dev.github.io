Unity Engine Mechanics

Mechanics: Follower

  • Capsules: Capsules follow the player, but they can't get too close to the player or each other.
  • Enemy Aggro: Initially, the enemy is in roaming mode. When it gets within a certain distance, it switches to chasing mode.
  • Movement: Player movement is controlled using Unity's New Input System.

Mechanics: Import of Mixamo Character

This mechanic involves importing a character from Mixamo, assigning a looping animation to it, and placing it in a 3D scene with a placeholder representing Mars. The character is positioned on a teleportation portal.

Mechanic: Hack n' Slash Prototype

  • Spawn: The character spawns with a superhero landing animation.
  • Character: Mixamo avatar imported, with added animations and a small sword in hand.
  • UI: The UI includes a front camera and a top-down camera view.
  • Movement: Movement is point-and-click.
  • NPC Interaction: When within range, the NPC waves to attract attention.
  • Cursor Transformation: The cursor changes when interacting with the NPC.
  • Dialogue Placeholder: A placeholder for a dialogue bubble appears.
  • NavMesh: The NavMesh allows movement on slopes.
  • Interaction: The cursor shape changes to show that you can interact with a panel.
  • Enemy Interaction: When hovering the cursor over the enemy, two small swords appear as feedback to indicate it's an enemy.
  • Enemy Selection: Once selected via a click, a placeholder image appears in the UI for the enemy.
  • Gizmos: As a developer, I display gizmos for detection.
  • Attack Mode: When approaching the yellow circle, the skeleton enters attack mode, moves towards us, and says, "I will kill you!"
  • Health Bars: A placeholder health bar appears above the skeleton’s head, and another under the player’s feet.
  • Sword Strikes: We hear the sword strikes.
  • Enemy Death: Once the skeleton is dead, it screams, "Aaaaargh!"
  • Chest Interaction: The camera rotates to show a small chest that is interactable.
